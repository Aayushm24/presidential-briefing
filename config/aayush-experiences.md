## Verified observations (things Aayush directly saw or experienced)

### LLM counting limitation (strawberry bug)

- **Context:** "i broke GPT — asked how many r's are in strawberry. it said 2. S-T-R-A-W-B-E-R-R-Y. there are 3."
- **Context:** "LLMs are like autocomplete on steroids — they predict patterns, not count characters. that's why 'how many r's in strawberry' breaks them."
- **Context:** "the fix: make it list positions first before counting, use code for counting, force independent double-checks"
- **Tags:** LLM behavior, tokenization, strawberry, hallucinations, agent debugging

### AI over-engineering observation (mom + dentist)

- **Context:** "my mom asked AI to cancel her dentist appointment. it wrote a LinkedIn message. 147 words. 3 paragraphs. 'i am writing to formally request...'"
- **Context:** "she deleted all of it. typed 'can't make Saturday.' sent."
- **Context:** "every AI tool is broken the same way — rocket engines on bicycles"
- **Tags:** AI UX, over-engineering, simplicity, agents doing too much
