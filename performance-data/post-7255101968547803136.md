> Last week was a huge win for me....
>
> I've been in SF for the past 3 months, grinding pretty much every day to build a strong HR community 
>
> As I leave in a few days, I wanted to organize one last meet-up with all the amazing HR folks I've met so far :)
>
> I am happy to report that it went AMAZING!!
>
> It was the first-ever event where either Sandeep Talukdar or I used 0 LinkedIn invites to invite people.
>
> (I kid you not; I now know you can only send 200 invites per week on LinkedIn)
>
> To keep the community engaged, we had 2 group discussions:
>
> 1. Flexible hours: Boosts productivity or encourages procrastination
> 2. Office perks: Engaging or gimmicky.
>
> When we first came here, building a community seemed such a huge task, I always wondered why someone would want to come down and meet us.
>
> Weird, how the universe works.
