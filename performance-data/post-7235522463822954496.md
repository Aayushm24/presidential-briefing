> It's so hard to be productive all the time...
>
> These past 2 weeks, I've worked my ass off in SF
>
> Many people have asked me what I have seen here so far
>
> When I tell them not much, they're usually surprised 😅
>
> I think people just see the fancier version of our time here
>
> But, what they don't see:
> - Lows when people say this problem isn't worth solving
> - Meeting 4-5 people daily, attending events 
> - Cleaning, cooking, household chores
> - Walking upwards of 8 Kms daily
> - A lot of self-doubt
>
> Don't get me wrong, I'm enjoying every bit of my time here
>
> Though grinding daily is hard, like really hard
>
> I'm a huge fan of what Naval Ravikant says:
>
> Knowledge workers function like athletes - train, and sprint, then rest and reassess
>
> So at this time, I'm training and sprinting 💪🏻
>
> Today has been another day with a lot of productive meetings:
>
> I started my day at 8 am 
>
> I had a meeting with Pranav, and we spoke about what he thinks of AI in customer support:
>
> - They've built an internal chatbot to manage customer support queries
> - The need for an AI tool becomes imp when you've 10+ customer support reps 
> - Dealing with nuances is critical, and the integrations with different sources of info like Discord, Slack, Intercom, etc should be stitched correctly.
>
> In the afternoon, met Daniel today and discussed what he thinks about AI in customer support:
>
> - Most orgs talk to customers using Slack connect channels, so can Albus read, categorize, and suggest answers by pinging the POC
> - As Albus is integrated with all org docs and tools, can it suggest documentation changes when it finds conflicting information
> - The overall idea is to improve documentation and get analytics on questions that are asked multiple times
> - This is relevant as smaller companies don't have their documentation in place, so if we can help them with that, they will get their Aha moment faster
>
> Time has flown by so quickly, I'm honestly looking to relax and catch up on sleep this weekend 💤
>
> Sandeep Talukdar and I are doing our 1st road trip this Sunday, super stoked about this 🚀
>
> Here's to another successful week of learning and growth 🥂
>
> p.s. saw Dolby's office today 🙌🏻
