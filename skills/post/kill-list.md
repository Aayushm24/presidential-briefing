# Kill List

Words, phrases, and patterns banned from every LinkedIn post. Checked during review. Any violation = automatic revise.

## Category 1A: Classic AI buzzwords
delve, testament, tapestry, landscape, paradigm, paradigm shift, ecosystem, synergy, synergies, holistic, empower, democratize, disrupt, disruption, disrupting

## Category 1B: Corporate fluff
crucial, robust, cutting-edge, seamless, leverage, utilize, utilize, optimize, streamline, scalable, innovative, best-in-class, world-class, next-generation, state-of-the-art, mission-critical, end-to-end

## Category 1C: 2025-2026 AI tells
"at its core", "plays a significant role", "it's important to note", "it's worth noting", "in today's rapidly evolving", "the future of", "in an era of", "as we navigate", "the intersection of"

## Category 1D: Overwrought emotion
breathtaking, extraordinary, unparalleled, remarkable, game-changing, game changer, groundbreaking, ground-breaking, revolutionary, transformative, transformational, unprecedented, monumental

## Category 1E: Transition crutches
"In fact", "Indeed", "Additionally", "Furthermore", "Moreover", "Notably", "Interestingly", "Importantly", "Essentially", "Fundamentally", "Ultimately"

## Category 1F: Cliche openers
"Imagine if", "Picture this", "Let me share", "Here's what I learned", "I've been thinking a lot about", "Let's talk about", "Can we talk about", "Hot take:", "Unpopular opinion:", "I'll say it:"

## Category 1H: LLM analogy patterns
"It's like [analogy]. [Short dramatic sentence]." — This is the most common LLM tell in explanatory writing. Instead of "It's like X," turn the analogy into a direct question to the reader.

## Category 1G: Structural patterns
- Em-dashes (use commas or periods instead)
- More than 1 emoji per post
- Hashtag spam (max 3, only at the very end)
- Bullet-point listicle format (no posts that are just a list)
- Starting 3+ sentences with the same word
- Rule of three (LLMs force groups of 3 — use 2 or 4 instead)
- Generic positive conclusions ("The future looks bright", "Exciting times ahead")
- Starting the post with "I"

## How to enforce

During the voice + kill list review pass:
1. Scan draft for every word and phrase above
2. Scan for structural patterns in 1G
3. Any match = specific revision instruction ("Replace 'leverage' with a specific verb", "Break up the three parallel sentences")
4. After revision, re-scan. Repeat until clean.
