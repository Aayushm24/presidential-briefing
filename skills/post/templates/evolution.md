# Template: Evolution

Use when the lead story extends a thread we've posted about before. Shows intellectual honesty — your thinking is growing.

## Structure

```
[Hook: reference to past post + what changed — under 120 chars]

[What you said before — 1-2 sentences. Be specific about 
 the claim you made and when.]

[What new evidence appeared — the lead story, with specifics.]

[How your thinking updated — not "I was wrong" (unless you 
 were actually wrong), but "Here's what I'd add now" or 
 "The picture is more nuanced than I thought."]

[The updated position — your new, more complete take.
 This should be richer than the original because it 
 incorporates more data.]

[Closing — what you're watching next on this thread.]
```

## Constraints

- Total: 900-1500 characters
- Must reference a specific past post or briefing topic
- Shows growth, not flip-flopping. There's a difference.
- Only use when you genuinely have a more nuanced position
