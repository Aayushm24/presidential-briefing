# Template: Absurd Mirror

Humor reveals truth. Setup something relatable, hit with something absurd, then extract the real insight.

## Formula

```
[relatable situation stated simply]

[build it up — what everyone does / experiences]:
- [detail 1]
- [detail 2]
- [detail 3 — the longest, most specific]

[the absurd twist — something ridiculous that's also true]

[let the humor breathe — a fragment or reaction line]

[the actual insight underneath the joke.
 this is where the education lives.
 one idea per line.
 specific and actionable.]

[closing — either a callback to the joke or a genuine question]
```

## Examples of the pattern

"you spent 14 years in school. then 4 years for engineering. then 2 years at Deloitte. then GMAT. then MBA. now you're working at a job you don't like. and somewhere a cat with Instagram is making more than your entire career."

"every day i see a new ai startup hit 10m ARR in 6 months with 4 engineers. the term ARR is getting redefined. it used to mean 'annual recurring revenue.' now it's 'artificially ridiculous revenue.'"

## Rules

- The absurdity must contain a genuine truth
- Don't explain the joke — trust the reader
- The educational payoff comes AFTER the humor, not before
- Keep it short. Absurd posts lose power when they're long.
- Dashes (-) not bullets
- lowercase i
