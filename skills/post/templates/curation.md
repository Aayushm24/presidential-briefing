# Template: Curation

Use when no single story dominates but multiple interesting items are worth sharing together.

## Structure

```
[Hook: pattern across multiple items — under 120 chars]

[The pattern — what connects these items? Don't just list them.
 Explain the thread that runs through all of them.]

[Item 1 — 2-3 sentences. What happened + the one takeaway.]

[Item 2 — 2-3 sentences. What happened + the one takeaway.]

[Item 3 — 2-3 sentences. What happened + the one takeaway.]

[What the pattern means — zoom back out. 
 Why should someone tracking AI care about this cluster?]

[Closing question — "Which of these are you paying attention to?"]
```

## Constraints

- Total: 900-1500 characters
- 2-4 items (not more, or it becomes a listicle)
- Items must share an underlying theme (not just "things that happened")
- Each item gets 2-3 sentences max
