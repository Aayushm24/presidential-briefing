# Template: Personal Discovery

Aayush's top-performing format. Vulnerability + education. "I broke something, learned why, and now you don't have to."

## Formula

```
I [did unexpected thing / made a mistake].
[Short line acknowledging it]

[What everyone does / what I was doing]:
- [thing 1]
- [thing 2]

then [what happened — the discovery moment].

the finding [strong emotional reaction]:
[the insight, stated simply]

[why this happens — the technical explanation, 
 broken into short lines, one idea per line.
 use "you and I" language for analogies.
 make it conversational, not academic.]

[what I changed — specific, from my real work.
 "i've been building X. the moment i stopped doing Y, Z happened."]

[the reframe — the memorable line.
 not generic wisdom. specific to this topic.]

[closing — permission-giving CTA or binary choice question]
```

## Rhythm

Short line.
Medium explanation.
Then the longest line carries the weight of the insight.

Back to short.

Always vary length. Never 3+ lines the same length in a row.

## Rules

- Open with confession/vulnerability, but pivot to education fast
- The technical explanation is the VALUE — don't skip it for more storytelling
- Include at least one specific from Aayush's real work ("i've been building multi-model pipelines")
- End with permission or a question that makes the reader examine their own behavior
- Dashes (-) not bullets
- Bold max 3-5 words in the whole post
- lowercase i throughout
- Every idea gets its own line
