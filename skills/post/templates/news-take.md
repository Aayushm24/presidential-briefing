# Template: News + Take

Use when something genuinely big happened and you have an original angle.

## Structure

```
[Hook: named entity + unexpected action — under 80 chars]

[What happened — 2-3 sentences, pure facts. No opinion yet.]

[Your take — this is where the conviction goes. State it directly.
 Not "I think maybe..." but "Here's what's actually happening."]

[The evidence — 2-4 short paragraphs. Each one connects a data point 
 to the conviction. Use brain connections here. Specific numbers.
 Named companies. Dated events.]

[The implication — what does this mean for someone building AI systems?
 1-2 sentences. Practitioner-focused.]

[Closing question — binary choice format. Forces the reader to pick a side.]
```

## Constraints

- Total: 900-1500 characters
- The take must come from the conviction, not from the article
- At least 2 data points from different time periods
- No external links in body (put in first comment if needed)
