# Template: Dot-Connecting

Use when the brain finds a BRIDGE connection — two previously unrelated topics that are actually about the same underlying phenomenon.

## Structure

```
[Hook: two seemingly unrelated facts juxtaposed — under 120 chars]

[Fact A — 1-2 sentences. Specific, dated, named source.]

[Fact B — 1-2 sentences. From a completely different domain.]

["Here's what connects them" — the synthesis. This is the 
 core value of the post. Explain the underlying principle 
 that both facts are evidence of. This connection should 
 be something nobody else has made publicly.]

[Why this pattern matters — what does it predict?
 What should a builder do with this insight?]

[Closing observation — not a question, just a clear statement 
 of the pattern. Let the reader sit with it.]
```

## Constraints

- Total: 900-1500 characters
- The two facts must come from genuinely different domains
- The connection must be non-obvious (if it's obvious, use news-take instead)
- This format only works when the brain has accumulated enough to find bridges
