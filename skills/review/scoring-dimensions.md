# Scoring Dimensions

5 dimensions, each 1-10. Used for LinkedIn post review. Be calibrated — a 7 is good, an 8 is great, a 9 is exceptional.

## 1. Novelty

Does this post say something new, or is it just a rephrased summary?

- 9-10: Original connection that nobody has made publicly. Uses brain connections to create unique synthesis.
- 7-8: Fresh angle on a known topic. You'd stop and think "I hadn't considered that."
- 5-6: Decent take but you've heard variations of it before.
- 3-4: Basically a summary of someone else's post with minor commentary.
- 1-2: Pure rehash. No original thought.

## 2. Insight density

How much do you learn per sentence? Is every sentence pulling its weight?

- 9-10: Every sentence adds new information or advances the argument. Zero filler.
- 7-8: 1-2 sentences could be tighter but overall dense with insight.
- 5-6: Mix of insight and filler. Some sentences just "sound good" without saying anything.
- 3-4: More padding than substance. Reading between the lines, there's maybe one insight.
- 1-2: All fluff. You reach the end and haven't learned anything.

## 3. Voice match

Does this sound like Aayush, or like an AI content generator?

- 9-10: You'd believe Aayush typed this himself. Casual, direct, specific, opinionated.
- 7-8: Close to his voice with minor adjustments needed.
- 5-6: Generic "smart person on LinkedIn" voice. Not bad, but not distinctly Aayush.
- 3-4: Clearly AI-generated. Smooth, polished, says nothing anyone would disagree with.
- 1-2: Full ChatGPT slop. Engagement bait, buzzwords, empty inspiration.

## 4. Hook strength

Would you stop scrolling to read this? Test: show the first line to someone and watch if they tap "see more."

- 9-10: Immediate tension. You MUST know what comes next. Specific, surprising, gap-creating.
- 7-8: Strong interest. You'd read more out of curiosity.
- 5-6: Mildly interesting but you might keep scrolling.
- 3-4: Generic. "Here's what I've been thinking about AI lately."
- 1-2: Active repellent. "Excited to share my thoughts on the future of AI!"

## 5. Teach-ability

Can someone walk away from this post knowing something they didn't?

- 9-10: Clear new mental model installed. "Oh, I never thought about it that way."
- 7-8: One solid takeaway that changes how you think about the topic.
- 5-6: Interesting but you wouldn't change any behavior or thinking.
- 3-4: Vague awareness that something happened. No concrete takeaway.
- 1-2: Empty inspiration. "AI is changing everything." No kidding.
