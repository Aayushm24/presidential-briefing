## Best option recommendation

**Option 1 (The Split)** is the strongest against the conviction brief.

Reason: it directly tests conviction bet #1 (binary split forces self-identification) AND conviction bet #3 (visual inequality) in a single frame. At 3-second read, the viewer gets: (1) two kinds of teams exist and (2) one side is visually dominant. The "9 mo" number lives inside the dominant panel as the earned conclusion — not leading with the stat, but landing it after the split does its work. The visual inequality is literal (the panels are unequal in width, the right panel fills with accent color, the typography inside the right panel is heavier). This is the one that will make a founder feel the dread — they will immediately self-identify with the gray side.

Runner-up: Option 4 (Fresh Sprint) — most editorial, most shareable, "9 months" is the clearest hero. Strong choice if the goal is maximum screenshot-and-share behavior.
