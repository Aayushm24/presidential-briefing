## Recommendation — winner

**Ship Option 5 ("The Final Question") as the single image.**

- Highest avg score (9.1).
- Makes the reader finish the creative with their own answer — the behavior the position statement asked for ("forward to co-founder"). A question demands an answer; a stat demands a nod.
- Passes all three success criteria without qualification.
- Lowest failure surface: no wrap issues, no panel balance risk, cleanest at 400px.

If he wants a **carousel** (LinkedIn supports up to 10 images), ship this order:
1. Option 3 ("The Countdown") — the hook, scroll-stop
2. Option 1 ("12 Months") — the number, deadline

4. Option 4 ("Absorbed") — the binary choice
5. Option 5 ("The Final Question") — the close, forward line

This maps to the post's structure and ends on the question — same ending as his text.

If he wants a **single image**, it's Option 5.
